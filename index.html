About us

From our kitchen to your table.

Bakery 99 is a small-batch bakery built on quality, creativity, and doing things the right way—everything is made from scratch, by hand, and with intention. What started as a one-woman kitchen has grown into a space for thoughtful baking, where every item is crafted fresh and never rushed.

From sourdough breads to pastries and seasonal drops, each bake is created with care, attention to detail, and a focus on flavor. No shortcuts, no mass production—just real ingredients and a process that puts quality first.

At its core, Bakery 99 is built on the belief that every bite should spark joy—designed to elevate everything from cozy mornings to grand celebrations.
